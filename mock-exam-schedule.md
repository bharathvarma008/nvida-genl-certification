## Mock Exam Best Practices

### Before Each Mock Exam
1. **Get Good Sleep**: 7-8 hours the night before
2. **Eat Well**: Good breakfast/lunch before exam
3. **Review Strategy**: 30 min review of exam strategy guide
4. **Prepare Environment**: Quiet space, no distractions
5. **Set Timer**: Use actual timer, simulate real exam

### During Each Mock Exam
1. **Time Management**: 
   - First 90 min: Answer confident questions
   - Next 25 min: Review flagged questions
   - Last 5 min: Final check
2. **Flag Strategically**: Don't flag everything, flag what you need to review
3. **Stay Calm**: Don't panic if you don't know an answer
4. **Answer Everything**: No penalty for wrong answers
5. **Trust Preparation**: You've studied hard, trust yourself

### After Each Mock Exam
1. **Immediate Analysis**: Review results while fresh
2. **Identify Weak Areas**: List domains <70% accuracy
3. **Review Incorrect Answers**: Understand why you got them wrong
4. **Update Progress Dashboard**: Track improvement
5. **Create Remediation Plan**: Plan focused study for weak areas
