## Remediation Best Practices

### 1. Focused Study Sessions
- **30 minutes minimum** per weak domain per day
- Review concepts first, then practice questions
- Review incorrect answers immediately

### 2. Targeted Practice
- Focus on weak domain questions
- Aim for 40-50 questions per weak domain
- Track accuracy improvement

### 3. Resource Review
- Re-read relevant sections from official resources
- Watch video explanations if available
- Review quick reference guide for that domain

### 4. Cross-Domain Connections
- Identify how weak domain relates to other domains
- Review related concepts from other domains
- Practice integrated scenarios

### 5. Progress Tracking
- Update accuracy after each practice session
- Track improvement over time
- Adjust remediation plan if needed
